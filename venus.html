Venus Missions

Venera 7 (1970)

Venera 7 was the first successful lander to transmit data from the surface of Venus, providing valuable information about its atmosphere and conditions.

Akatsuki (2015-Present)

Akatsuki is currently studying the atmosphere of Venus, focusing on its cloud dynamics and weather patterns.

This mission aims to study Venus' atmosphere and determine its composition and origin.
