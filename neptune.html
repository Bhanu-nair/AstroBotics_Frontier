Neptune Missions

Voyager 2 (1989)

The only spacecraft to visit Neptune, Voyager 2 provided incredible data about the planet's atmosphere, rings, and moons.

No Current Mission

Currently, there are no active missions to Neptune. Scientists continue to analyze Voyager 2 data.

Proposed Mission: Neptune Odyssey

A proposed NASA mission to study Neptune’s atmosphere and its moons, particularly Triton.

Trident (Proposed)

Trident, a proposed mission, aims to explore Neptune’s largest moon Triton, investigating its surface and potential for life.
